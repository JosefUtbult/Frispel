Become a member

Hello, and welcome to Frispel!

To become a member in Frispel you need to do the following.

  1. Create an account at this site. This will require you to fill in your LTU Student Ideal in the format xxxxxx-n. If you don't have an ideal, DONT FILL IN A NONE EXISTING ONE. In this case, contact the board and we'll go through what you need to do to get one.
  2. Pay the membership fee of 500 kr for a years membership or 300 kr for half a years membership (if not another fee has been specified). This fee needs to be paid to our postal giro number 713632-8. Please write your name in the text field on your payment. If you aren't able to pay via postal giro, contact the board and we'll figure out another means of payment.
  3. Sign our membership agreement, which includes an agreement to follow our rules of procedure. You can get a physical document by contacting the board, or print one yourself from here. After signing, you can scan it yourself and send it to the board, or contact the board to leave it to us in person.
  4. Contact the board and tell us that you've done the previous steps. Then we will activate your account and you will get access to book our music room.

Contact the board

If you need to contact the board, feel free either email us on frispel.ordforande@gmail.com, or message us at our facebook page. If any of these steps is unclear, don't hesitate to write to us.
